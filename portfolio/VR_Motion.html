Virtually Motionless:

Unreal | VR

Overview:

In addressing the challenges of motion sickness in Virtual Reality (VR), I embarked on a comprehensive exploration that culminated in the creation of a prototype VR project using Unreal Engine.

This endeavor involved an in-depth investigation into the causes and effects of motion sickness, leading to the implementation of innovative techniques to mitigate these discomforts. Focusing on the seated VR experience, my project introduces a dynamic dash rotation system, adaptive field of view adjustments, a nuanced Niagara particle system for establishing a stable point of reference, and a complete digital embodiment approach. By seamlessly integrating these elements, the prototype aims to redefine the VR encounter, offering users a more comfortable and immersive experience while pushing the boundaries of technological solutions for motion sickness within virtual environments.

Features:

  • Dynamic field of view shader
  • Custom dash control system.
  • Digital embodiemnt.
  • Original sound design.
  • Niagara particle system.
