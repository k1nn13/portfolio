Cyber Drive:

Unreal | C++ | Dev Log

Overview:

Embarking on the creation of 'Cyber Drive,' a game set in a Blade Runner-inspired world, I am immersed in the dynamic realm of game development using Unreal Engine and C++.

The focal point of this project lies in the nuanced and exhilarating movement of the meticulously designed in-game vehicle, inspired by the iconic Blade Runner aesthetics, with the ongoing development process serving as a log of my endeavors and discoveries in the realm of game design.

LOG 1 | Pitch & Roll:

In the initial phase of the 'Cyber Drive' project, I've created a detailed custom model using Maya and implemented four physics thrusters inspired by drone mechanics. This design enables precise control over the in-game vehicle's movements, emphasizing agility and immersion.


Additionally, I've integrated a PS4 controller, allowing dynamic adjustments to individual engine thrusts for seamless control over pitch and roll. This marks the beginning of an exciting journey into game design, with more innovations on the horizon.
