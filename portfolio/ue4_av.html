Live Audio-Visual Performance

Unreal Engine

Design:

Using UE4 I developed a custom live audio visual performance tool exploring audio reactive generative design and shaders. During the research phase of this project I experimented with different frameworks and libraries including MAX for Live, Super Collider and openFrameworks.

Key Features

  • Live audio analysis and smoothing in UE4.
  • Recieve and process OSC data from Ableton Live to UE4.
  • Generative Design with audio interaction.
  • Niagara Particle System with audio interaction.
  • Reactive HLSL custom shaders (.usf) exploring ray marching.
