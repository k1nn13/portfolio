Unreal | PoseAI

Overview:

In this ongoing project, I am harnessing the power of PoseAI to meticulously capture and translate body movements into real-time motion data for Unreal Engine.

The integration of PoseAI not only facilitates precise body tracking but also adds a layer of authenticity to the project by transforming these movements into dynamic motion capture data.


Collaborating with a dancer, the development process is an immersive journey that explores the synergy between technology and artistic expression. The utilization of Niagara particle systems further enhances the visual aesthetics, creating stunning effects that harmonize seamlessly with the fluidity of the dancer's movements.


As a work in progress, this project embodies the intersection of cutting-edge technology, artistic collaboration, and the exciting potential of real-time motion capture in a dynamic and evolving digital space.
