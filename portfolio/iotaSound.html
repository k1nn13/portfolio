Generative Sound Design

In a collaborative project I worked with fellow student Tom Benyeunes to create a game using openFrameworks. We chose to use particle systems and fluid simulation as the main graphical feature delivering a relaxing and deep experience. This project was created during the pandemic and it was necessary to communicate remotely to discuss direction, planning, production and development. My role in this project was to create a generative sound track and sound design to help the player feel a sense of progression. Using synthesis techniques to sequence generative audio and adding sound effects such as filter and delay related to player positioning allowed smooth development of the soundtrack, this helped to embrace a consistant and immersive experience. Working as part of a team was essential to this project and effectively communicating helped to deliver a complete demo.
